Our Story

Knight's Code was founded in 2021 amid the COVID-19 pandemic in response to the flurry of workers moving to remote work. Knight's Code has stepped up to help small and medium businesses equip themselves for the technological demands of running their businesses from anywhere in the world. We pride ourselves on the ability to customize and provide support for ALL of our customers cyber needs.
